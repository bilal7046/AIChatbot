AI-Powered Virtual Assistant Demo

Welcome to Our AI Chatbot Demo

This is a demonstration of our multilingual virtual assistant widget. The chatbot is embedded as a floating widget on this page and supports both text input and press-to-speak voice input.

Try asking about:

  • Navigation: "Where are you located?" or "How do I get to your office?"
  • Services: "What services do you offer?" or "What can you help me with?"
  • Status: "What's the status of my request?" or "When will it be ready?"

How to Use the Chatbot

Text Input: Click the chat button in the bottom-right corner, type your question, and press Enter or click Send.

Voice Input: Click and hold the microphone button to speak your question. Release when finished.

Note: Voice input requires a modern browser with microphone access. You may need to allow microphone permissions.

About This Demo

This MVP demonstrates a fully functional AI chatbot widget that can be easily embedded into any website. The widget is self-contained and uses a knowledge base to provide intelligent responses to user queries.

The chatbot handles three main categories of inquiries: navigation guidance, service explanations, and status inquiries. All responses are generated from a predefined knowledge base that can be easily updated.
