=== STATUS INQUIRIES ===

How to Check Status:

Order/Request Status:
You can check the status of your order or request through multiple channels:
- Online Portal: Visit our website and log in to your account. Go to "My Requests" section.
- Email: Send an email to status@jmminnovations.com with your request number
- Phone: Call our support line at +1-234-567-8900 and provide your request ID
- Chat Support: Use our live chat feature on the website

Processing Times:
- Standard Requests: 2-3 business days
- Priority Requests: 1 business day (additional fee applies)
- Complex Projects: 5-7 business days (you'll receive regular updates)

Status Types:
- Pending: Your request has been received and is in queue
- In Progress: Our team is actively working on your request
- Under Review: Your request is being reviewed by our quality team
- Completed: Your request has been completed and is ready
- On Hold: Your request is waiting for additional information

Tracking Your Request:
Each request is assigned a unique tracking number (format: REQ-XXXXXX). Use this number to track your request status.

Notifications:
You will receive email notifications at key milestones:
- When your request is received
- When work begins
- When your request is completed
- If additional information is needed
